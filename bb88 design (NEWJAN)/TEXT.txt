ABOUT US:
BB 88 Advertising & Digital Solutions Inc. is a full-service creative 
agency that offers a comprehensive range of services including graphic 
design, architectural design, realistic visual production, as well as web 
and mobile application development. As a one-stop shop, the company 
focuses on crafting high-impact visual solutions that effectively 
communicate and sell products and services. Their goal is to help turn 
creative ideas into strategic communication tools tailored to each 
client’s target market.

Driven by a passion for delivering the right message at the right time 
with the right solution, BB 88 combines the expertise of professionals 
from various creative fields with advanced technology. By closely 
collaborating with clients, the team develops distinctive branding and 
marketing strategies that work across print, video, radio, and digital 
platforms—ensuring a strong, consistent presence in today’s fast-
evolving media landscape.

Our Team:

Software Engineers:
These skilled professionals design, build, and maintain the 
software systems that drive our digital world, using their 
expertise in programming and databases to deliver reliable 
solutions

App Developers:
App Developers build user-friendly mobile apps for iOS and 
Android using C#, combining technical skill with a deep 
understanding of the mobile ecosystem

Web Developers:
These experts build and optimize websites and web apps 
using technologies like HTML, CSS, MySQL, PHP, AJAX, 
Bootstrap, and JavaScript, ensuring seamless design, 
functionality, and mobile compatibility

Graphic Artists:
Graphic Artists are the creative minds behind the visuals that 
make up our digital world. They use software like Photoshop 
and Illustrator to create stunning images, graphics, and 
animations that help bring websites and apps to life

Creative Writers:
Creative Writers and Editors craft clear, on-brand content for
